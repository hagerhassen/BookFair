International London Bookfair Awards

We love to celebrate the outstanding talent within our industry here at LBF. Our awards ceremonies range from those celebrating international excellence
in all fields of the publishing industry, recognising the innovative trailblazers who are under 30 and taking the industry by storm and an award that
recognises those taking the publishing industry online via book blogs, Instagram and book vlogs.

International Excellence Awards

Held in partnership with The Publishers
Association, the International Excellence Awards
were held on the evening of Tuesday 10 April
2018 at Olympia and celebrated publishing and
related activities outside of the UK.

Find out more

The UK Book Blog Awards

The UK Book Blog Awards @ LBF was a new
initiative for 2018 designed to celebrate social
media influencers and their important
contribution to the book industry.

Find out more

The Trailblazer Awards

Powered by LBF and in partnership with
BookBrunch and the SYP, the Trailblazer
Awards celebrate those under 30 innovating
and trailblazing their way through publishing.
