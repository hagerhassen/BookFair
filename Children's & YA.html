Children's & YA

Always one of the most colourful areas of the show, Children's & YA traditionally welcomes the leading names in the field to exhibit.

Each year, these companies enjoy buzzing aisles full of visitors, providing the opportunity to increase brand awareness, generate new business leads both domestically and globally, and increase their networks.

Children's Hub

As part of the Insights Seminar Programme, we have a dedicated stream titled the Children's Hub featuring sessions that take place across the three days of the Fair.

Find Children's Exhibitors

The Children's & YA area at LBF is a vibrant hub of business and is always the most colourful area of the Fair. Each year, we see the leading companies from the sector come to the Fair to do business with our 25,000 attendees.

Big Name Children's Authors
