Conferences During the Fair


1-The Quantum Conference


In partnership with Nielsen Book, we surveyed our global audiences to elicit the themes people want addressed in 2018. Armed with this insightful data, we are creating a compelling programme delivered by quality speakers. Themes include publishing trends and tactics, new audiences, and business models.

2-Introduction to Rights

Aimed at those just starting out, Introduction to Rights is an invaluable half-day conference. It offers the skills and knowledge needed to make the most of every rights opportunity, vital in this ever-changing publishing and content creation environment. Find out more

3-What Works? Education Conference

What Works? Education Conference examines the transition to digital learning technologies, publisher resources for educators, and developments in copyright.

4-Research & Scholarly Publishing Forum

The Research & Scholarly Publishing Forum tackles global trends in Research & Scholarly Publishing, and provides a platform for international publishers to learn, improve business practice, and develop new models and markets.


Children's Media Conferences During the Fair


1-Nielsen Book UK Children's Summit 2018

Hosted by Nielsen Book in collaboration with The London Book Fair, this summit covers the latest research into children’s books and reading, revealing key information on the role of books in the rapidly changing media landscape that children are having to navigate. Other sessions will explore building empathy through reading; understanding the multicultural children’s book consumer and making the most of backlist.

2-CMC Exchange @LBF

CMC Exchange @LBF gives attendees the chance to meet potential partners in broadcasting and children’s content production. This innovative market day allows attendees to build new relationships using our super-efficient "Meeting Mojo" booking system to organise focused one-to-one meetings.

Other Conferences


1-The Writer’s Summit

The Writer's Summit was a day designed to help budding, beginner and hybrid writers make informed decisions and develop successful writing careers.

2-Building Inclusivity in Publishing

Building Inclusivity in Publishing returned for its second year this November. The conference addressed the issues around the importance of inclusivity and diversity in publishing.
