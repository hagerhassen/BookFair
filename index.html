CAIRO INTERNATIONAL BOOK FAIR

The Cairo International Book Fair is the largest and oldest book fair in the Arab world, held every year in the last week of January in Cairo, Egypt, at the Cairo International Fair Grounds in Madinat Nasr. The Fair is considered the most important event in the Arabic publishing world. 

Address : Cairo Governorate, Egypt
          Al Estad, Nasr City.



Duration : 26 Jan 2017-10 Feb 2017



Open Hours : 9:00am - 7:00pm
