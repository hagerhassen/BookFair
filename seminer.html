Cairo International Exhibition Seminars 2018

Dar Al-Shorouk Publishing House will organize 20 book signing ceremonies at the exhibition's headquarters in Cairo, during the days of the exhibition which will be held from January 27 to February 10, 2018.


The following is the program of the signing of Dar Al-Shorouk at the Cairo Book Fair 2018:


1-On Tuesday, January 30th, at 4 pm, the signing ceremony of the book Nightingale and Cinderella will be held by Ashraf Gharib.


Wednesday, January 31, the ceremony of signing the book of the ghosts of Beit Heinrich Bell, by writer Alaa Khaled, will be held at 2 pm.

On February 1, the signing ceremony of Barqash 's book without barriers will be held by the writer Abdullah Al - Sanawi at 2:00 pm On the same day, the signing ceremony will be held for the writer and the press colleague on the seventh day, It is 4 pm.


On Friday, February 2, the signing ceremony of a book close to the delight, by Ahmed Samir, will be held on Friday, February 2. On the same day, the writer Anisa Hassouna will sign her book without warning, at 3:30 pm.



Alexandria International Book Fair 2018

1-The Alexandria International Book Fair will continue its activities for the fourth day. The activities will begin Tuesday (April 3rd) from 11:00 am at the 3rd Egyptian International Festival of Arabic Poetry, organized and presented by the poet Dr. Khairi Al Silkawi.


2-At 1:00 pm, a workshop on "Data Journalism and Future Challenges" will be presented by Dr. Ihab Hamdy, Professor of Media at the University of Alexandria.



3-In the second evening, under the title "Humanitarian participants and their impact in support of peaceful coexistence", Dr. Mohieddin Afifi, Secretary General of the Islamic Research Academy, and presented by Dr. Ahmed Attieh.



4-At 3 pm, a two-day workshop was held for Dr. Dalal Makari Baouch, Director of the International Drama Without Borders Institute, entitled "A World of Paper and the Playwright of the Book".




5-At 4:30 pm, a lyrical poetry evening with a drawing of the gum, in the plaza square at the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, poetry is given by Mohammad Ramadan al-Kafouri, the singing of Abdullah Salem and the drawing of the artist Mohammed Shehab.

6-In parallel, the Alexandria International Book Fair celebrates the Spring of Poets in cooperation with the Supreme Council of Culture through an evening in which Dr. Ahmed Darwish, Ragab El Sawy, Mohamed Farid Abu Saada, and a number of poets from Alexandria will present it.



7-And at the fourth and a half in parallel, a technical evening for the late artist and musician Mohamed Afifi, entitled "Continuity of generations on the anniversary of the birth of the late musician Mohamed Afifi" in which Dr. Osama Afifi, and a selection of artists of Alexandria.



8-In the fourth and a half, in parallel, a symposium entitled "The Fourth Revolution" on the technology revolution, in which Dr. Loay Abdul Majid, presented by Dr. Yusri al-Jamal, former Minister of Education.



9-And at 7 pm, a technical symposium entitled "Diving in the music of the nineties" in which the artist Hisham Nour, and presented by the writer Ahmed Bassiouni.



10-In parallel, a symposium entitled "One hundred years on the Balfour Declaration .. Readings in the advertiser and silent about him" in which the journalist Muhsin Abdul Aziz, the writer and researcher Maysara Salahuddin, and Dr. Nada Yusri Professor of Hebrew literature Alexandria, and presented by Hazem Abu Saudi Arabia, followed by the signing ceremony of the book "The Balfour Declaration" to the facilitator Salahuddin
