Why exhibit at LBF?

The London Book Fair's international reach is breathtaking. More than 60 countries regularly exhibit, visitors come from 118 countries and there are around 1,500 global countries represented.

Exhibiting opportunities are occasionally offered through the Association, often in partnership with international book trade fairs. Members are alerted to such opportunities through the bulletin and other announcements. However, the Association no longer manages an extensive regular program of combined exhibits at academic conferences. Alternative providers of such cooperative exhibit services include: The Scholar's Choice, Combined Book Exhibit, and Association Book Exhibit.

The Fair welcomes over 25,000 attendees from more than 118 countries. Anyone who is involved with the creation, distribution, sale or treatment of content. Authors, talent scouts, editors, designers, digital gurus walk the floor, meeting, talking, observing, and discovering.
